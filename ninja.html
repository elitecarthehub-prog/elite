How to Use

1

Power On

Insert the key into the ignition and turn right to power on the body. Press the handlebar start button to activate sound and lights.

2

Throttle & Wheel

Twist the throttle on the right handlebar to activate rear wheel rotation. The dynamic linkage gives a realistic, hands-on riding feel.

3

Smoke Effect

Open the fuel tank cap, fill with the included water container, and inject into the tank. Activate to see simulated exhaust smoke spray.
