How to Use

1

Straighten

Turn the appliance ON and push the styling switch to the leftmost position. Glide the plates slowly through sections of hair for smooth, sleek results.

2

Crimp

Press the plates together and push the switch to the rightmost position. Clamp sections of hair and hold briefly, then release for textured crimped waves.

3

Curl

Press the plates together and bring the switch to the middle to lock. Wrap a section of hair around the barrel, hold for a few seconds, then release for defined curls.
